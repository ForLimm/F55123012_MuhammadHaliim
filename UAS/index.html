Tips & Actions

Reduce Plastic Usage

Switch to reusable bags, bottles, and containers.
Gunakan tas, botol, dan wadah yang dapat digunakan kembali.

Save Energy

Turn off lights and devices when not in use.
Matikan lampu dan perangkat saat tidak digunakan.

Choose Public Transport

Reduce vehicle emissions by using public transport.
Kurangi emisi kendaraan dengan menggunakan transportasi umum.

Plant Trees

Engage in reforestation efforts.
Ikut serta dalam upaya penghijauan.

Recycle Waste

Sort and recycle your waste materials.
Pilah dan daur ulang bahan limbah Anda.

Support Renewable Energy

Use renewable energy sources whenever possible.
Gunakan sumber energi terbarukan jika memungkinkan.
